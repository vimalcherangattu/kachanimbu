I am Vimal. This is my space to share thoughts and ideas on anything that crosses my mind.

I got tired of using AI for everything - even personal messages - and I had begun losing the knack to write properly. This is my attempt to keep that part alive in me.

This is an outlet full of contradictions, weirdness, and mistakes. But this is me. Anything I write won't be perfect, won't be great - it'll be average. And average is awesome.
